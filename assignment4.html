After talking to the persona, the problem statement of the project is: how might we make a university student who enjoys taking film photos to document his life address the problem of searching, verifying, and organising fragmented film information among various platforms so that he can save time and have an easier film taking experience?

Therefore, this website aims to provide one-step trustful information with functions and content including product information and authentic reviews. It is positioned as an e-commerce platform plus a community for film photography beginners with enthusiasm, here you can view products and guides, and join the film discussion and offline activities. The key assumption is that users think the content on the website is useful and attractive, so they would like to register for it. In assumption, the attractive points contain 1. useful membership-only discussion 2. Attractive member-only discount 3. Attractive member-only offline activities. In this way, visitors are willing to pay for membership.

For more information about the business model, please check my journal for more information.
